The application is designed to provide accommodations for students who have learning disabilities but it can also be used for helping English Language Learners. para3: The final application was designed specifically for the iPhone. With that being said, the app could be easily ported for use on a tablet or Android phone in order to reach a wider range of users. Keeping in mind the needs of the user, the primary goal of the design for application was to not distract the user with unnecessary design elements but to keep things simple so they are able to focus on the content itself. para4: The colours used in the design were a pastel bluish colour, an off white colour to mimic the look of physical paper and a dark brown colour for all the copy. White was not a dominant colour in the design because it would be too distracting and could potentially make reading the text on a device more challenging. method-1: Persona method-2: Journey Map method-3: Storyboard method-4: Wireframe next-project: Refresh Website nexturl: work/refresh researchpara: For many children in the education system learning is not very easy.
