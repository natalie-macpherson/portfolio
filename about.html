About

“Design changes the way people look and respond to the world around them.”

Hey, my name is Natalie May Macpherson and I am a graphic designer. I live in Ottawa, Ontario, Canada but I am always willing to work with people who do not live in the same city as me.

Graphic design is a huge passion of mine. It enables me to be creative and solve problems in an innovative way. I put a lot of commitment into my work and I take great pride in that.

I love the process of making stuff. It's so rewarding when you start a project from a crazy idea to something that is well developed and the client and audience really likes it. I love the idea that I can design something which can make somebody's life easier. Overall, in many ways, design changes the way people look and respond to the world around the.
