Just as Angela does in her work the book uses type and imagery to rewrite history by integrating typography into the pieces. para4: In creating this quote book linocuts were used for the title of the book and the initial capitals for each letter used. For each letter used a historical figured was picked along with a quote of historical significants that they stated.
